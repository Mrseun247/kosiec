About KOSIEC

The Kogi State Independent Electoral Commission (KOSIEC) is the statutory body constitutionally established to conduct, monitor, and supervise all LGA elections in Kogi State, Nigeria.

Established in 2004 and headquartered at No. 1 Ado Ibrahim Road, GRA, Lokoja, KOSIEC is led by Hon. Chairman Mamman Nda Eri, with a dedicated team of 25+ electoral professionals.

Our mandate extends across all 21 Local Government Areas — covering Chairmanship and Councillorship elections for all wards, ensuring grassroots democracy at every level.

  • ✔Conduct Chairmanship elections for all 21 LGAs
  • ✔Conduct Councillorship elections for all wards
  • ✔Voter registration and state voter register management
  • ✔Civic education and electorate sensitization
  • ✔Accreditation of political parties for LGA elections
  • ✔Official publication and certification of election results
🎯

KOSIEC delivers comprehensive electoral management services to ensure credible local government democracy in Kogi State.

Chairmanship Elections

Full organization and supervision of LGA chairman elections across all 21 LGAs from voter accreditation to final result declaration.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is KOSIEC and what does it do?
+
KOSIEC stands for Kogi State Independent Electoral Commission. Established in 2004, it is the statutory body responsible for conducting, monitoring, and supervising all LGA elections in Kogi State — covering Chairmanship and Councillorship elections across all 21 LGAs.
Who is eligible to vote in LGA elections?
+
Any Nigerian citizen who is 18 years or older, resident in Kogi State, and duly registered as a voter is eligible to vote. You must present a valid Permanent Voter Card (PVC) on election day.
How do I register to vote in Kogi State LGA elections?
+
During designated registration periods, eligible residents visit their LGA KOSIEC office with valid ID. Watch for official KOSIEC announcements on registration dates and locations.
How can I check election results?
+
Official results are published on this website under "Results" following every election. They are also displayed at LGA collation centers. For certified copies, contact our Lokoja office.
How do I contact KOSIEC?
+
Phone: 09169490757 or 08035904252. WhatsApp: 09169490757 (24 hours). Email: info@kosiec.org. Physical office: No. 1 Ado Ibrahim Road, GRA, Lokoja. Office hours: Weekdays 9am–5pm.
How do political parties participate in LGA elections?
+
Parties must be registered with INEC at federal level and comply with KOSIEC's accreditation requirements. They must conduct primaries within the stipulated period and submit candidate credentials before the filing deadline.
What should I do if I witness electoral malpractice?
+
Report immediately to the nearest KOSIEC officer at the polling unit, your Ward Collation Officer, or contact the Commission's office. KOSIEC takes electoral malpractice very seriously.
How many LGAs does KOSIEC cover?
+
KOSIEC covers all 21 LGAs: Adavi, Ajaokuta, Ankpa, Bassa, Dekina, Ibaji, Idah, Igalamela-Odolu, Ijumu, Kabba/Bunu, Kogi, Lokoja, Mopa-Muro, Ofu, Ogori/Magongo, Okehi, Okene, Olamaboro, Omala, Yagba East, and Yagba West.
